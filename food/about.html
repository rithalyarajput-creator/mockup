Our Story

We Are the Daddy of All Tastes

Founded in 2019, Daddy of Tastes started with a single obsession — creating food so bold, so flavorful, that one bite would change your entire day.

From a small kitchen in downtown New York, we've grown to serve over 50,000 happy customers across the city, staying true to our roots: real ingredients, real flavor, real fast.

Our Core Values

Everything we do is guided by these principles

🌿

Fresh Ingredients Only

We source locally and seasonally. No frozen shortcuts, no artificial fillers. Every ingredient is chosen for maximum flavor and freshness.

⚡

Speed Without Compromise

Fast delivery doesn't mean cutting corners. Our optimized kitchen workflow ensures your food arrives hot, fresh, and on time — every time.

❤️

Made with Passion

Our chefs are food lovers first. Every recipe is crafted, tested, and perfected until it makes us say "that's it — that's the Daddy flavor."

♻️

Sustainable Practices

Eco-friendly packaging, reduced food waste, and partnerships with local farms make us better for the planet — not just your plate.

🤝

Community First

We give back to the neighborhoods we serve through local hiring, food donation programs, and supporting small farmers.

🔒

Safety & Hygiene

Our kitchens exceed health standards. Regular audits, trained staff, and strict hygiene protocols keep every order safe and clean.
