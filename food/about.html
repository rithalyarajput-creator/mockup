Our Story

We Are the Daddy of All Indian Tastes

Founded in 2019, Daddy of Tastes began with a single obsession — creating Indian food so bold and authentic that one bite would transport you to your grandmother's kitchen.

From a small kitchen in Mumbai, we've grown to serve over 50,000 happy customers, staying true to our roots: real ingredients, real recipes, real fast delivery.

Why Us

Our Core Values

Everything we do is guided by these principles

Always Fresh

Every dish is cooked to order. No pre-cooked batches, no reheating. Your food is prepared the moment you place your order.

Speed Without Compromise

Fast delivery doesn't mean cutting corners. Our optimized kitchen delivers hot food at your door in under 35 minutes.

Made With Passion

Our chefs are food lovers first. Every recipe is crafted, tested, and perfected until it makes us say "that's the Daddy flavour."

Local & Sustainable

We source from local farms and suppliers. Eco-friendly packaging, minimal waste, and a commitment to our community.

Community First

We hire locally, support small farmers, and run food donation programs to give back to the neighbourhoods we serve.

Safety & Hygiene

FSSAI certified kitchens, regular audits, trained staff, and strict hygiene protocols on every order every time.
