FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What are your delivery hours?

We deliver 7 days a week from 10am to 11pm. Delivery may vary on public holidays.

What is the minimum order?

Minimum order is ₹199 for delivery. No minimum for pickup. Free delivery on orders above ₹499.

Do you accept UPI / GPay?

Yes! We accept all UPI apps (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM), all cards, net banking, and COD.

Do you offer catering?

Yes! We cater events and offices. Contact us at least 48 hours in advance with your requirements.

Is the food 100% vegetarian?

Yes! Our entire current menu is 100% pure vegetarian. We use a dedicated veg-only kitchen.

Wrong item received. What now?

We'll make it right! Contact us within 30 minutes and we'll redeliver or offer a full refund.
