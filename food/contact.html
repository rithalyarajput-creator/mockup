FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What are your delivery hours?

We deliver 7 days a week from 10am to 11pm. Holiday hours may vary — check the app for updates.

What is the minimum order?

Minimum order is $10 for delivery. No minimum for pickup orders.

Can I modify my order?

Yes, you can modify your order within 2 minutes of placing it. After that, the kitchen is already working on it.

Do you offer catering?

Absolutely! We cater events, offices, and parties. Contact us at least 48 hours in advance for catering inquiries.

Is there a loyalty program?

Yes! Every $1 spent earns 1 point. 100 points = $5 reward. Download the app to start earning today.

What if my order is wrong?

We'll make it right! Contact support within 30 minutes and we'll redeliver or refund — no questions asked.
